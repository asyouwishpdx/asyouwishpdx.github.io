The Boucher Room is named In honor of Francois Boucher, my Great-Uncle who was a famous French Painter in the Rococo Art Movement. His Niece, my Mother, has her art group meet here every Tuesday morning at 10:00 AM. Daylight fills the room and makes the comfy window seat very inviting. Large enough for a group of 12 seated adults.
